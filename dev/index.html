Star Graph is a site that creates co-stardom networks between film actors.

Add an actor by typing his or her name in the top left field. Remove an actor by right-clicking his or her node.

You can also remove all instances of a movie by right clicking any of its edges.

The site uses jQuery and Cytoscape.js to create the graph.

All data and images come from The Movie Database.

Contact stargraphio@gmail.com with questions, comments, suggestions, or if you just want to chat.
